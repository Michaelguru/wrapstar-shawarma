The Wrapstar Shawarma Story: From Campus Craving To Local Favorite

Published on: June 02, 2025 | By: Wrapstar Team

Every great business begins with a simple idea sparked by a craving — and Wrapstar Shawarma is no exception. Nestled right inside the Kwara State University campus in Malete, Wrapstar started as a humble passion project aimed at satisfying one thing: delicious, mouthwatering shawarma and more for hungry students and locals alike.

See More...

The Birth of Wrapstar

The founder, Otuole Emmanuel Eze, noticed a gap in quality, affordable, and tasty fast food options around campus. Determined to fill that gap, he combined his love for flavorful street food with a commitment to fresh ingredients and excellent customer service. What started as a small stall soon grew into a beloved spot known for its juicy shawarma, hand-crafted burgers, crispy fries, and refreshing ice cream.

What Makes Wrapstar Different?

At Wrapstar Shawarma, it’s not just about quick food — it’s about creating an experience. Every wrap and burger is carefully prepared, using locally sourced ingredients to ensure every bite bursts with flavor. The team believes fast food shouldn’t mean compromising on quality, and their loyal customers couldn’t agree more.

A Community Hub

More than just a food spot, Wrapstar Shawarma has become a vibrant community hub. It’s where students catch up, friends hang out, and families enjoy a casual meal together. The welcoming atmosphere and friendly staff make it a place you want to return to again and again.

Looking Ahead

Wrapstar is constantly evolving, always listening to customer feedback and exploring new menu ideas. With plans to expand and bring their signature flavors to even more people, this local favorite is just getting started.

Visit Wrapstar Today!

If you haven’t tried Wrapstar Shawarma yet, you’re missing out on one of Malete’s best-kept secrets. Drop by the Kwara State University campus, or check out their menu online and order via WhatsApp — your taste buds will thank you!
