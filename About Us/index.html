Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Wrapstar Shawarma located?

Wrapstar Shawarma is located within the Kwara State University campus in Malete, Ilorin, Kwara State.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Sunday from 12 PM to 10 PM. On Sundays, we have a brief break from 6 PM to 9 PM.

What types of food do you serve?

We specialize in delicious shawarma, hand-crafted burgers, crispy fries, and creamy ice cream desserts.

Can I place an order online?

Yes! You can place your order through our website by browsing the menu and clicking the "Make Order" button, which redirects you to WhatsApp for order confirmation and payment.

Do you accept online payments?

While we don’t support direct online payments on the website yet, we offer a convenient "Pay 4 Me" feature that allows secure payment through WhatsApp.

What is the "Pay 4 Me" feature?

It’s a secure way to have someone else pay for your order. You share a payment link with the payer, and once the payment is completed, we process your order.

Can I customize my order?

Yes, you can specify any customizations or special requests when placing your order via Website.

How long does delivery or pickup take?

Delivery and pickup times vary depending on demand and location. Please contact us via WhatsApp or phone for exact timing.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer a variety of options including veggie shawarma and plant-based burgers.

How can I contact Wrapstar Shawarma for inquiries or orders?

You can reach us at +234 915 768 4982 via WhatsApp or call us directly. Alternatively, send an email to wrapstarhub@gmail.com.
